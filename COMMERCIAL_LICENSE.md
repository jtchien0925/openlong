## FAQ

**Q: I'm a researcher at a university. Do I need a commercial license?**
A: No. Academic research is fully covered by the AGPL license at no cost.

**Q: I want to use OpenLong in my company's internal pipeline (not redistributed).**
A: Internal use without redistribution or SaaS exposure is covered by AGPL.
However, if your modifications are served to external users, AGPL applies.

**Q: I'm building a cloud genomics platform and want to include OpenLong.**
A: You need a commercial license unless you open-source your entire platform under AGPL.

**Q: Can I try OpenLong before purchasing a commercial license?**
A: Yes. The AGPL version is fully functional. Evaluate freely, then contact us
when you're ready to integrate commercially.
